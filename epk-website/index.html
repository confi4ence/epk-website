THE ARTIST

Mr Shane SA (real name Shane Sepheu) is a South African DJ and producer originating from Ga-Masemola, a small township in the Sekhukhune region of Limpopo.

What started as a passion for rhythm and sound has grown into a serious presence in the South African house music scene. Known for his soulful, dance-oriented tracks, Mr Shane SA brings energy, authenticity, and deep groove to every performance.

His signature sound blends deep house, Afro tech, and soulful dance music crafted for both the club and the soul. He performs on Pioneer equipment and delivers sets that move crowds from intimate lounges to main stages.

Mr Shane SA is also a key part of 1060 Sounds, frequently collaborating with artists like Recky R and other rising talents in the scene.
